About us

About The Talent Acceleration Group

What We Do

TAG develops strong leaders, grows organizations, and enhances team effectiveness. Our custom approach identifies your talent-related strengths and opportunities, aligning people with business strategy through services like executive coaching, leadership development, and talent management.

How We Work

We partner with you to assess leadership potential, place the right leaders in the right roles, and support them as they step into greater responsibility. We streamline talent processes like succession planning and focus development resources on your next generation of high-potential leaders.

Our Experience

With 20+ years of experience, TAG has served clients in biotech, finance, retail, tech, energy, and nonprofits. We've worked internally and as consultants, partnering with senior leaders and HR teams to build high-impact, sustainable leadership strategies — and enjoying the process along the way.
